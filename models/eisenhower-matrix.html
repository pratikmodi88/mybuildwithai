The Eisenhower Matrix is a simple tool for prioritizing tasks and managing workload. It helps you distinguish between what is urgent and what is important, allowing you to focus on the work that actually moves the needle.

How to use this today:

List everything on your plate. Force-rank them into these 4 boxes. If it's in the Delegate box, find someone else to do it immediately. If it's in Eliminate, cross it off and don't look back.
