The 'Why'

The Eisenhower Matrix is a simple tool for prioritizing tasks. It helps you distinguish between what is urgent and what is important.

Practical Application

Force-rank your tasks into these 4 boxes. If it's in the Delegate box, find someone else to do it immediately.
