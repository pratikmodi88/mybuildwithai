The AI Customer Service Revolution

How Klarna transformed their customer experience from 11-minute waits to 2-minute resolutions using a digital AI teammate.

01

The Pain: Support Logjams

Global shopping giant Klarna faced a massive challenge: thousands of customer queries daily, leading to long wait times, overworked staff, and high operational costs. Human agents were bogged down by repetitive questions like "Where is my refund?"

02

The Spark: The AI Teammate

Instead of just a basic chatbot, they deployed a sophisticated GenAI assistant. This "digital employee" was trained to handle full conversations, access real-time data, and resolve issues end-to-end without human intervention.

03

The Win: Speed & Scale

The results were immediate. The AI assistant now does the equivalent work of 700 full-time human agents, resolving issues in less than 1/5th of the original time while maintaining high customer satisfaction.

$40 Million Annual Profit Improvement

"This isn't just about saving money; it's about shifting human intelligence up the value chain. By automating the 'boring' repetitive tasks, the organization can refocus its people on complex problem-solving and innovation. This is the blueprint for the AI-First Enterprise."
