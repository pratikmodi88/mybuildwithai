The Situational Leadership® II Model

Published: February 24, 2026 • 5 Min Read • Category: Management & Strategy

Diagram adapted from Ken Blanchard, 1985.

The 'Why'

Effective leadership is not a "one size fits all" approach. The SLII® model provides a framework for leaders to adapt their style based on the development level of the individual they are leading on a specific task.

Key Components

  • Directing (S1): High directive, low supportive behavior.
  • Coaching (S2): High directive, high supportive behavior.
  • Supporting (S3): Low directive, high supportive behavior.
  • Delegating (S4): Low directive, low supportive behavior.

Practical Application

To use this model, first diagnose the individual's development level (Competence + Commitment) for the task at hand. Then, match your leadership style (S1 through S4) to provide exactly what they need to succeed.
